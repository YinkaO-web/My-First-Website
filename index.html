The Best characters in A Hat in Time

There are a multitude of characters in the 2017 indie game "A Hat in Time". Though who is the best? who is the worst, and who are equally liked and despised to a point where the overall opinion of the character is pretty ok? Here is my top tier list of all of the characters in a hat in time from least liked to adored, so we can at least figure out my opinion.

A Hat in Time is an indie RPG collectathon that focuses on a 12 hat year old (in human years she's 168) that uses hourglasses to power her ship to get back home. After an intruder broke her front window of the ship, her Time pieces (magical hourglasses with the potential of granting power and powering her spaceship) flew into a foreign world filled with anti-heroes. These characters we will be inspecting today.

Background

You play as the characters "Hat Kid" or "Bow Kid" (if multiplayer is applied).

/>

They have powers such as Double jumping, Wall jumping, Homing attacks, using various magical hats with special powers and using badges for extra powers. Their goal is to retrieve and protect the Time pieces, and to stop any anomalies caused by Time rifts (when the Time piece is broken). When traversing a time rift, they may come across storybook pages. If you collect them all, it'll tell you the backstory of main characters. As of now, Time pieces may be referred as "TP".

6th: "Mad Crow"

The Mad Crows are very annoying, especially at the beginning of the game. They are annoying because when they notice you, they will deliberately come to attack you. Luckily in A Hat in Time, each attack takes one heart pon (health points) out of 4, so you'll have a chance to get away.You might ask,"Yinka! Cut them some slack. This is not really something to be angry over." and I'll kindly say that you haven't heard/seen nothing. There are special levels called time rifts which are caused by Timepieces breaking. In some of those levels, there are types of mad crows which just get on my nerves so much! They wear a johnny bravo hairdo and is extremely fast. While the normal mad crows would slowly hop to you giving you some time to run away, these gremlins will run at you at inhumanely posssible speeds.

5th: Sleepy Raccoons

These little goobers are adorable but can also be a hassle. They are enemies commonly found in Mafia town, Subcon Forest, and remote Timerifts. They travel by floating from their snot bubble. Once the player is under one, the snot bubble pops causing the raccoon to slam onto the ground -waking it up- causing 1 damage. It'll soon go back to sleeping so you don't have to worry about a cranky raccoon. Some benefits of this character is that they can help with platforming. By partaking a Homing attack, it can help with getting to hard to reach places.

4rd: "Mafia Boss"

The Mafia Boss is the rough and tough leader of the mafia and the first boss in the game. He -as other bosses- is after your Time Pieces; wanting to use them for personal benefits. His motives of obtaining your "TP" is for power and he shows off that power in his boss fight. After you beat him, what remains of him (because of Mustache Girl who is referred to very soon) is a a pair of eyeballs and a brain in a jar.

3nd: "Mafia Goons"

The Mafia Goons are the identical, broken english, russian accent bearing denizens of Mafia Town. Their customs are goofy as heck as some ride on giant rolls of anime meat and they own a HQ/Theater/Restaurant that is on top of an infinite geyser. Now they are not the sharpest tools in the shed and the kindest people as they can be pretty rude. But their silly antics and vibrant culture inspired me to put them as third.

2nd:"Mustache Girl"

Mustache Girl is the main Antagonist of the game and has a goal of collecting all of the time pieces to abolish crime and makea justice-based authoritarian system. You might be wondering "why she is 2nd if she is the bad guy?" and the reason is her backstory. When ou hae an antagonist in a game, there is a high chance that they will become disliked by the players depending on the severity of their actions. Mustache girl was after power but was trying to do a little good by doing a little bad. Later on, the player learns of Mustache Girl's backstory. Turns out the Mafia took over the the land and mistreated the indigenous people. This led Mustache girl to have a great hatred for Villains (which is a majority of the characters). Its not hard to feel bad for her once you realize that her livelihood was forever impacted by the Mafia's reign.

1st:"Cooking Cat"

Cooking cat is number one because just look at her. She is a southern accent speaking, plantigrade cat that (secretly) makes the food for the Mafia. Her backstory actually kinda reminds me of Ratatouille which makes her even better.
